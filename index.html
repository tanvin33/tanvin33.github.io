Hi, I'm Tanvi! I'm a first-year Master's student in Computer Science at Princeton University. Previously, I majored in Computer Science at Cornell University, where I was advised by Jon Kleinberg.

At Cornell I was a part of the Milstein Program in Technology and Humanity, through which I worked on a junior capstone project with the Digital Due Process Clinic. I was also deeply involved with the Cornell CIS community as VP of the Academic Team for Women in Computing at Cornell (WICC).

I spent the summers of 2022 and 2023 working as a Software Engineering Intern at Slack. I spent my time there working with the Bedrock (Kubernetes) and Datastores teams.
